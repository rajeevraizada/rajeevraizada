Research questions

  • How are neural representations structured, and how do they underlie behaviour?
  • Individual differences in behavioural performance.
  • Social and environmental effects on learning.

Methods

  • Multivoxel pattern-based fMRI analysis (MVPA).
  • Computational modeling.
  • Psychophysics.
  • EEG brain-computer interface (BCI).
