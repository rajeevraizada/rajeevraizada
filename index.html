Welcome to the Raizada lab, in the Department of Brain & Cognitive Sciences at the University of Rochester.

Research questions

  • How are neural representations structured, and how do they underlie behaviour?
  • How does the brain represent linguistic meaning?
  • Possible parallels between the brain's computations and machine learning

Methods

  • Multivoxel pattern-based fMRI analysis (MVPA)
  • Computational modeling
  • Behavioural psychophysics
